My experiences teaching were undoubtedly the highlight of my undergraduate experience at Michigan. Meeting new students and watching them grow not only as students but as individuals was extremely fulfilling.

I was fortunate to contribute to the learning experiences of my peers in various roles. I served as an M-Write Fellow for Stats 250: Introduction to Statistics and Data Analysis, and a peer tutor for several introductory Computer Science courses including Elementary Programming Concepts and Data Structures and Algorithms.

For three semesters, I served as a Teaching Assistant for EECS 370: Introduction to Computer Organization.
